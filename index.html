STATES

India's states exhibit diverse cultural heritages. Rajasthan features vibrant folk music and dance, while Kerala showcases classical arts like Kathakali. Tamil Nadu offers classical music and stunning temples. Punjab boasts lively Bhangra and the Golden Temple. These states exemplify India's rich and multifaceted cultural tapestry.

UNION TERITORIES

India's union territories (UTs) are unique entities with distinct cultural and administrative characteristics. For example, Delhi, a UT with a special status, is the nation's capital and a political hub. Meanwhile, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Lakshadweep are tropical paradises known for their pristine beaches and marine biodiversity.
