LAKSHADWEEP

Lakshadweep is an Indian union territory comprising coral atolls and islands in the Arabian Sea. Known for its stunning coral reefs, clear waters, and serene beaches, it's a secluded paradise for nature enthusiasts and water sports enthusiasts.

DELHI

Delhi, India's capital, is a bustling metropolis rich in history and culture. It features historic landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and modern attractions such as shopping malls and vibrant street markets.

ANDAMAN

Andaman and Nicobar Islands, located in the Bay of Bengal, are an Indian archipelago known for pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and diverse marine life. It's a popular tourist destination.
